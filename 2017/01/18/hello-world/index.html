My Food Porn

Hi everybody, or whoever is reading, thanks for checking out my blog - My Food Porn! If you like home cooked, comfort food from all over the world, I think you will love my food! I like to experiment with any kind of food,
including many types of Asian (Vietnamese, Japanese, Chinese, etc.) as well as Italian (I’m part Italian, so it kind of makes sense I guess lol) as well as Southern food (I’m from Georgia), so no matter what kind of food you
like you will probably see something here you like (at least I hope so) with simple, easy to follow instructions on how you can make these meals yourself! Keep note though; my recipes are bare bones.
They aren’t exact by any means because my personal philosophy is that crafting food is not only an art but an expression of a culture.
I want people who see these pictures to take stock of what’s said and seen and take it and run wild with it.
Take it and make it your own, because food is every human being’s right.

I hope to post pretty regularly, at least twice a week, if possible. I absolutely cannot wait to share my food with you and I sincerely hope
you enjoy reading about it as much as I enjoy cooking it!

Also, if you would like to see some pictures (this is My Food Porn right (:) feel free to check out my gallery here.

Thanks again!
